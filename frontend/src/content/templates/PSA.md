# Professional Services Agreement Summary

This agreement establishes terms between a Customer and Provider for professional services delivered via Statements of Work (SOWs).

## Services

Provider performs work per SOWs, with change order procedures for modifications. Deliverables are deemed accepted if not rejected within the specified period.

## Intellectual Property

Deliverables transfer to Customer upon assignment, with Provider retaining Pre-Existing Materials rights. Customer owns materials it provides; Provider may use Feedback and Usage Data freely.

## Payment

Fees are invoiced per SOW, non-refundable except for specific termination refunds. Customer has a dispute period to contest charges while paying undisputed amounts.

## Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach (with 30-day cure period), insolvency, or Force Majeure events exceeding 30 days. Agreement continues until 12 months after the latest SOW term ends.

## Liability

Cumulative liability capped at General Cap Amount for standard claims, Increased Cap Amount for Increased Claims. Damages waiver excludes lost profits and consequential damages, with Unlimited Claims exceptions.

## Indemnification

Mutual defense obligations for Covered Claims, with procedural requirements including prompt notice and cooperation.

## Confidentiality

Recipient must protect disclosed information using reasonable care, with permitted disclosures to employees and advisors under confidentiality obligations.

## General Terms

- Governing Law applies to all interpretations and disputes
- Neither party may assign without prior written consent
- Parties are independent contractors
- No third-party beneficiaries
- Agreement may be signed in counterparts

## Definitions

- **"Agreement"** means the Standard Terms and any Order Forms or SOWs.
- **"Applicable Laws"** means all laws, rules, and regulations that apply to a party.
- **"Deliverables"** means the work product to be delivered under a SOW.
- **"Fees"** means the amounts specified in a SOW.
- **"Feedback"** means suggestions or comments about the Services.
- **"Force Majeure Event"** means an unforeseen event outside a party's reasonable control.
- **"Provider"** means the party providing professional services.
- **"SOW"** or **"Statement of Work"** means a document describing the Services, Deliverables, timeline, and Fees.
- **"Standard Terms"** means these Professional Services Agreement Standard Terms.
- **"Usage Data"** means data about the provision and use of the Services.
